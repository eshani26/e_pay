1. Advance Booking

Cancellations must be submitted at least 48 hours before the booking date to receive a refund.

A 1-hour maintenance buffer is automatically applied after your checkout time.

The auditorium must be left in its original condition. Damage costs will be deducted from the deposit before return.

6. Mandatory Charges

A Service Charge of 15% on selected optional facilities and a Refundable Deposit of Rs. 15,000 are mandatory conditions â€” not optional checkboxes. They are applied automatically to every booking.

7. Weekend Bookings

Weekend bookings (Saturday & Sunday) are permitted but require approval from the Deputy Post Master General.

8. Facility Requirements

Multimedia equipment setup requires advance notice. Canteen services require a minimum of 50 attendees.
